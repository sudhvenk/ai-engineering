# Athletics & Strength Training

Athletics activities focus on land-based cardiovascular conditioning, strength training, and functional movement to improve endurance, muscle mass, and overall physical performance.

## BOOT CAMP / BOOT CAMP BURN
High-intensity circuit training combining cardio and strength.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Strength, endurance, calorie burn  
**Ailments:** Metabolic slowdown, muscle loss  

## CARDIO STRENGTH
Balanced cardio and resistance training in timed formats.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 3–5/week  
**Benefits:** Heart health, functional strength  

## DUMBBELL / SURGE / UPBEAT STRENGTH
Weight-based strength training targeting major muscle groups.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 45–75 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Bone density, muscle tone  
**Ailments:** Osteopenia, weakness  

## CYCLING
Indoor cycling simulating hills and endurance rides.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Cardio capacity, leg strength  

## SENIOR CIRCUITS
Functional strength training designed for older adults.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–5/week  
**Benefits:** Balance, daily movement safety
