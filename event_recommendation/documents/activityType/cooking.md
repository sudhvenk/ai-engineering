# Cooking & Culinary Activities

Cooking activities develop practical life skills, nutrition awareness, creativity, and cultural appreciation through hands-on food preparation.

## BEGINNER COOKING
Foundational cooking skills including knife work and basic recipes.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 60–90 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Independence, confidence  
**Ailments:** Poor nutrition habits  

## BAKING
Preparation of breads, pastries, and desserts.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 90–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Precision, creativity  
**Ailments:** Stress (therapeutic effect)  

## HEALTHY COOKING
Cooking focused on balanced nutrition and wellness.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 60–90 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Cardiovascular health, weight management  
**Ailments:** High cholesterol, poor diet  

## VEGETARIAN / VEGAN COOKING
Plant-based cooking techniques and meal planning.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–90 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Digestive health, sustainability  
**Ailments:** Inflammation, dietary imbalance  

## ETHNIC CUISINE
Exploration of global culinary traditions.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 90–120 min | **Frequency:** Occasional  
**Benefits:** Cultural awareness, creativity  

## MEAL PREP
Batch cooking for efficiency and nutrition planning.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–120 min | **Frequency:** Weekly  
**Benefits:** Time management, consistency  
**Ailments:** Irregular eating habits  

## COOKING FOR KIDS
Child-friendly cooking emphasizing safety and fun.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** Occasional  
**Benefits:** Skill-building, family bonding  

## CULINARY TECHNIQUES
Focused skill classes such as sauces, grilling, or knife mastery.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 90–120 min | **Frequency:** Project-based  
**Benefits:** Skill refinement, confidence
