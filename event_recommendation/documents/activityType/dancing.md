# Dancing Activities

Dance activities combine rhythmic movement, coordination, balance, and cardiovascular exercise. They also offer strong cognitive and social benefits through pattern learning, memory, and group interaction.

## LINE DANCING
Structured choreographed dance performed in lines without partners.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Coordination, memory, balance  
**Ailments:** Balance issues, mild cognitive decline, social isolation  

## BALLROOM DANCING
Partner-based dances such as waltz, foxtrot, or tango emphasizing posture and coordination.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 60 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Balance, posture, social engagement  
**Ailments:** Poor posture, low confidence  

## SALSA / LATIN DANCE
High-energy partner or group dances with fast footwork and rhythmic movement.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Cardio fitness, agility, coordination  
**Ailments:** Low stamina, stress  

## HIP HOP DANCE
Urban-style dance emphasizing dynamic movement, strength, and rhythm.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Strength, cardio endurance, confidence  
**Ailments:** Sedentary lifestyle, low energy  

## CONTEMPORARY DANCE
Expressive dance blending modern, jazz, and ballet techniques.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 60 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Flexibility, expression, body awareness  
**Ailments:** Movement stiffness, stress  

## CARDIO DANCE
Music-driven fitness dance designed primarily for cardiovascular conditioning.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Heart health, calorie burn  
**Ailments:** Weight management challenges  

## SOCIAL DANCE
Casual group or partner dancing emphasizing enjoyment and interaction.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–90 min | **Frequency:** Occasional–Weekly  
**Benefits:** Social connection, coordination  
**Ailments:** Loneliness, mild anxiety  

## CULTURAL / FOLK DANCE
Traditional dances reflecting cultural heritage and storytelling.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 60 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Cultural engagement, coordination  
**Ailments:** Social isolation, low motivation
