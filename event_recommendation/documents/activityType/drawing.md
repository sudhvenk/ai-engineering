# Drawing & Visual Arts

Drawing activities emphasize visual perception, creativity, fine motor skills, and focused attention. These activities are low physical impact but high cognitive engagement.

## BEGINNER DRAWING
Introductory drawing focusing on basic shapes, shading, and observation.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–90 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Creativity, confidence, hand-eye coordination  
**Ailments:** Stress, low confidence  

## SKETCHING
Quick, expressive drawing capturing form and motion.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Observation skills, mindfulness  
**Ailments:** Mental fatigue, stress  

## CHARCOAL DRAWING
Drawing using charcoal to explore contrast and texture.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 90–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Expressiveness, visual depth  
**Ailments:** Creative blocks  

## PENCIL DRAWING
Detailed drawing emphasizing line quality and shading.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Precision, patience  
**Ailments:** Attention difficulties  

## INK DRAWING
Drawing with ink focusing on bold lines and composition.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Confidence, decision-making  
**Ailments:** Creative hesitation  

## FIGURE DRAWING
Drawing the human form from observation or reference.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 90–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–2/week  
**Benefits:** Anatomy understanding, observation  
**Ailments:** Visual-spatial challenges  

## DIGITAL DRAWING
Drawing using tablets and digital tools.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 60–120 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Technical skills, creativity  
**Ailments:** Skill stagnation  

## ILLUSTRATION
Creating narrative or conceptual visual artwork.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 90–180 min | **Frequency:** Project-based  
**Benefits:** Storytelling, creativity  
**Ailments:** Creative burnout
