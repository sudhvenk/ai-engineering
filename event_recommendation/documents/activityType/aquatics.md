# Aquatics Activities

Aquatics activities are water-based exercise programs that use buoyancy and water resistance to reduce joint stress while improving cardiovascular health, strength, flexibility, and balance. These programs are widely used for rehabilitation, senior fitness, and low-impact conditioning.

## AQUA CARDIO
A water-based cardiovascular workout using continuous movement to elevate heart rate with minimal joint impact.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Cardio endurance, circulation, calorie burn  
**Ailments:** Joint pain, low stamina, back discomfort  

## AQUA CARDIO DANCE
Dance-style choreography combined with water resistance for rhythm-driven cardio.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Coordination, balance, enjoyment  
**Ailments:** Balance issues, mood decline  

## AQUA FIT
Full-body aquatic workout combining aerobics, toning, and resistance.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Muscle tone, endurance, joint mobility  
**Ailments:** Arthritis, osteoporosis risk  

## AQUA INTERVALS
Alternating high-effort bursts with recovery phases in water.  
**Intensity:** Moderate–High | **Session:** 30–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Metabolism, fat burn, cardio capacity  
**Ailments:** Poor cardio fitness, insulin resistance  

## AQUA RUMBA
Latin-inspired water dance focused on fun and light cardio.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 30–45 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Mood, coordination, social engagement  
**Ailments:** Low motivation, joint stiffness  

## AQUA ZUMBA®
High-energy Zumba choreography adapted for water resistance.  
**Intensity:** Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Cardio fitness, calorie burn  
**Ailments:** Joint sensitivity, stress  

## ARTHRITIS AQUA FITNESS
Gentle aquatic exercise designed for arthritis and rheumatic conditions.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 30–45 min | **Frequency:** 2–5/week  
**Benefits:** Pain reduction, mobility, confidence  
**Ailments:** Arthritis, fibromyalgia  

## WATER BARRE
Ballet-inspired movements adapted for aquatic resistance.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–3/week  
**Benefits:** Balance, posture, core stability  
**Ailments:** Balance instability, weak core  

## AQUA YOGA
Traditional yoga postures adapted to water for support and relaxation.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–4/week  
**Benefits:** Flexibility, relaxation, balance  
**Ailments:** Stress, chronic pain  

## AQUA YIN YOGA
Long-held passive stretches performed in water for deep release.  
**Intensity:** Low | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 1–3/week  
**Benefits:** Recovery, joint health, sleep quality  
**Ailments:** Chronic stiffness, anxiety  

## AQUA PILATES
Pilates-based core strengthening using water resistance.  
**Intensity:** Low–Moderate | **Session:** 45–60 min | **Frequency:** 2–3/week  
**Benefits:** Core strength, posture  
**Ailments:** Lower back pain, balance issues
