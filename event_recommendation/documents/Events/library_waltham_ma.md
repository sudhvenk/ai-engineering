# PAGE 1 — CENTER OVERVIEW
## Waltham City Library
**Type:** Library  
**Location:** Waltham, Massachusetts  

Waltham City Library is a community-focused public library that supports lifelong learning, creative exploration, and practical skill development. The library serves adults, seniors, families, and teens through hands-on workshops, arts education, and culinary literacy programs.

Facilities include a community meeting room, art workshop space, demonstration kitchen, and accessible learning areas. The library emphasizes small-group instruction and inclusive programming.

---

# PAGE 2 — PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Waltham City Library emphasizes **creative arts and practical cooking education**. Programs are designed for beginners and hobbyists, with a focus on skill-building, relaxation, and community connection.

Primary activity categories include:
- Drawing and illustration for beginners and intermediate learners
- Cooking classes focused on fundamentals, healthy habits, and meal preparation

Programs are suitable for adults, seniors, and motivated teens. Most classes are low-intensity, instructional, and discussion-based.

# PAGE 5 — COMMUNITY & LOGISTICS
**Registration:**  
Most programs require advance registration due to limited seating. Walk-in registration may be available if space permits.

**Accessibility:**  
The library is wheelchair accessible and offers seating accommodations. Cooking classes can adapt recipes for dietary restrictions upon request.

**Typical Attendance:**  
Class sizes range from 8–16 participants to ensure individualized instruction.

**Seasonal Scheduling:**  
Programs are offered in winter, spring, and fall cycles, with lighter schedules during summer months.
