# PAGE 1 — THEATRE OVERVIEW
## Velarium Cinema House
**Type:** Theatre  
**Location:** Worcester, Massachusetts  

Velarium Cinema House is a historic theatre reimagined as a modern cultural venue. Located in downtown Worcester, it offers curated screenings, educational film series, and moderated discussions for regional audiences.
