# PAGE 1 — CENTER OVERVIEW
## Summit Reach YMCA
**Location:** Pittsfield, Massachusetts  

Summit Reach YMCA is a regional wellness and youth development center serving Berkshire County families, teens, and seniors. The center emphasizes youth fitness foundations, aquatics for all ages, and active aging programs, while also offering limited creative and culinary enrichment.

Facilities include an indoor pool, gymnasium, group fitness studios, and multipurpose classrooms with accessibility features.
