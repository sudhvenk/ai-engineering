# PAGE 1 — CENTER OVERVIEW
## Harborlight YMCA
**Location:** Salem, Massachusetts  

Harborlight YMCA is a coastal community wellness and youth development center serving children, teens, families, adults, and seniors across the North Shore. The YMCA places strong emphasis on youth fitness, teen engagement, and family-friendly recreation, while also supporting adult wellness and senior mobility.

Facilities include a heated indoor pool, youth activity rooms, group fitness studios, multipurpose classrooms, and fully accessible locker facilities.

# PAGE 5 — REGISTRATION & LOGISTICS
**Registration:**  
Online registration is required for most programs. Youth and aquatics classes fill quickly and require advance sign-up.

**Accessibility:**  
The YMCA offers pool lifts, accessible locker rooms, and age-appropriate class modifications. Youth programs include enhanced supervision.

**Seasonal Scheduling:**  
Programs follow Winter, Spring, Summer, and Fall cycles with youth-focused offerings expanded during school breaks.
