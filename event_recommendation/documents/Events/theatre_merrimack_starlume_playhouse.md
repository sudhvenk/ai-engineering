# PAGE 1 — THEATRE OVERVIEW
## Starlume Playhouse
**Type:** Theatre  
**Location:** Merrimack, New Hampshire  

Starlume Playhouse is a boutique regional cinema serving the Merrimack Valley. The theatre focuses on accessible cultural programming, combining classic films, international cinema, and community discussion nights.
