# PAGE 1 — THEATRE OVERVIEW
## Opaline Grand Cinema
**Type:** Theatre  
**Location:** Boston, Massachusetts  

Opaline Grand Cinema is a premium art-house theatre located in downtown Boston. The venue is known for curated film programming, elegant interiors, and thoughtfully facilitated post-screening discussions. It serves adults, students, and cultural enthusiasts.
