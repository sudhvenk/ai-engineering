# PAGE 1 — CENTER OVERVIEW
## Seabrook Commons YMCA
**Location:** Plymouth, Massachusetts  

Seabrook Commons YMCA is a coastal community recreation center serving families, youth, and seniors in the Plymouth area. The YMCA places strong emphasis on aquatic safety, youth fitness, teen programs, and healthy aging, with light creative and culinary offerings.

Facilities include a heated pool, youth activity rooms, fitness studios, and multipurpose classrooms.
